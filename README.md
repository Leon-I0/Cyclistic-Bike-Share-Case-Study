🚲 Cyclistic Bike-Share Case Study

## Project Overview
This project analyzes historical ride data from Cyclistic Bike-Share to identify behavioral differences between annual members and casual riders.
The objective is to provide data-driven recommendations that support Cyclistic's marketing strategy to increase annual memberships.

## Business Problem
Cyclistic aims to increase annual memberships because members generate more stable and predictable revenue than casual riders.
The analysis focuses on understanding how both customer segments use the service and identifying opportunities to convert casual riders into annual members.

## Key Insights
- Annual members generated approximately 64% of all recorded trips.
- Casual riders travel longer distances and have longer trip durations.
- Members primarily use the service for commuting.
- Casual riders mainly use the service for recreational purposes.
- Member riders show consistent commuting patterns, while casual riders present higher seasonal variability.
